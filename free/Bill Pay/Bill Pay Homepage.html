Clinician Teaching Guide

Using This With A Patient

This simulator gives patients a safe, fictional utility bill-pay space to practice real-world payment skills before returning to a real account. Start with the support level that matches the patient's current needs, then increase difficulty as accuracy, confidence, and independence improve.

1. Set The Task

Ask the patient to sign in, find the current bill amount, review account details, and submit the practice payment.

2. Model The Process

Demonstrate scanning the page, reading labels, comparing numbers, and checking information before clicking submit.

3. Fade Cues

Begin with verbal or visual prompts, then reduce help so the patient practices sequencing and problem-solving.

4. Grade The Challenge

Use Easy for direct support, Medium for reference use, Hard for high distraction, and Realistic for pop-ups and web clutter.

5. Practice Safety

Discuss fake versus real payment sites, double-checking amounts, protecting card details, and asking for help.

6. Debrief

Review what went well, where the patient got stuck, and which strategy they can use during real bill payment.
