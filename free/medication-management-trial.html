Medication Management Trial

A fictional practice task for reading labels, organizing routines, and checking medication schedules.

Practice only. Do not use real medication names, real prescriptions, or real health information.

Task Instructions

  1. Identify which medication needs a refill request.
  2. Circle the medication taken twice daily.
  3. Write the safest time of day for Cardiwell.
  4. Check whether Sleepra is scheduled or as-needed.
