Why It Exists

Real-World Practice
For Independent

Patients recovering from stroke and brain injury need structured practice with the digital tasks of daily life. STRIVE INDEPENDENCE gives clinicians turnkey environments for communication, money management, community mobility, and executive function training.

Functional task practice
Banking, bill payment, AAC communication, and ride booking — the real tasks patients need outside therapy.
Cognitive rehab goals

Low adoption friction

Speech therapists can use STRIVE Talk and the real-world task modules to support AAC, aphasia communication practice, functional language, problem solving, memory strategies, attention, and caregiver-supported communication routines.
